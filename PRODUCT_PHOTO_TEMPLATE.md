# Product Main Photo Template

Use this brief whenever a raw artwork photo needs to become the main listing image.

## Visual Direction

Create a photorealistic hero product shot for a handmade calligraphy or painting artwork. The final image should feel authentic, elegant, and gallery-ready while preserving the actual painted surface, brush edges, paper or canvas texture, ink density, seal marks, and small handmade irregularities.

## Image Generation Brief

Use the uploaded artwork photo as the source product. Extract the full artwork from the random background, correct perspective so the art is squared and naturally vertical, normalize uneven lighting, and preserve all real brushwork, line texture, paper/canvas grain, pigment variation, edge softness, signatures, and seal details.

Place the artwork in a refined warm gallery product scene: soft natural side light, calm neutral wall or premium tabletop, subtle realistic shadow, gentle depth, no busy props. The artwork should be the clear subject, centered with generous negative space, photographed like a collector-grade original. Make it look hand-painted and authentic, not digitally printed or overly smooth.

Avoid changing the actual artwork design. Avoid fake text, new symbols, extra frames unless requested, plastic shine, harsh shadows, dramatic angles, clutter, watermark, stock-photo styling, or overprocessed AI texture.

## Output Rules

- Main shop image: 4:5 vertical or square crop.
- Keep the artwork fully visible.
- Preserve true colors unless the raw photo has bad lighting.
- Show enough surface detail to prove handmade quality.
- Use one consistent background family across the catalog: warm ivory, soft stone, muted walnut, ink-black accent.
- Use secondary images for close-up texture, edge/detail, and room context.

## Website Placement

Save final main images in `assets/products/` and use them as the first image in each product card or product detail page.
