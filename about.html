About the gallery

Harf-e-Noor is a hand-painted calligraphy art studio.

The gallery creates contemporary calligraphy works for collectors, homes, gifts, ceremonies, and quiet spaces of reflection.

Philosophy

Not decoration first. Meaning first.

The studio treats each word as a visual and emotional object. A successful piece should feel legible in spirit even when the form becomes abstract, gestural, or meditative.

Original handworkNo mass reproductions as originals
